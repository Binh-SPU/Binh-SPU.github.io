Intro

My full name is Binh Pham Nguyen Thanh and this is my Portfolio Website! You could call me Ben. Currently, I am a Junior studying Computer Science at Seattle Pacific University expected to be graduated in 2024. In my productive free time, I usually enjoy researching and learning to programming in different fields to develop my personal project collection, such as App Development, Game Development, and other similar projects. Here is some of my awesome projects so far!!!.

For more information, I am an international student from Vietnam. I have arrived and lived in America for over 3 years straight now. After that period, my endgoals have never changed ever since - that is to become an excellent developer, a better and independent individual. There are so much to learn and enjoy in this world. The excitement of learning new knowledge and making new connections is a driveforce that never faded in me even in the face of the pandemic. Overall, I am looking forward to what the future has installed for me.
